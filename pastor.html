Biography

Rev. Gary L. Wyatt, Sr. started playing and composing music prophetically at the age of 16 years old. Over the years thousands have experienced salvation, deliverance, restoration and healing through his fresh anointing, sincerity, and genuine ministry. He has ministered in the same conferences with Bishop TD Jakes, Andrae Crouch & The Disciples, Kirk Franklin, Fred Hammond, The Winans, BeBe & Cece Winans, The Clark Sisters, Commissioned, Yolanda Adams, The Kurt Carr Singers, and many others. In addition to his pastoral ministry, Rev. Wyatt has recorded one single entitled "Continue" that skyrocketed to #1 on Seattle's KNHC Gospel Showcase, and 25 solo albums (Genuine Joy, Impromp2, Values, NCCC Live, Proper Love, In Full Bloom, Verses & Choruses, GameHype 2000, Its My Time, I Do Love You, Secret Closet, The Decade, 25 Years Later, Born to Worship, Distinction, Eleven 2 Twelve, Impeccable Worship, Legacy, Love & Lullabies, Let's Celebrate, For the Crisis, Thirty, For Little Ears Only, Global Gospel, Alive to Sing Praise & Recovery), 23 of which were recorded on his independent label, Gold Glove Group, which was established with the assistance of former Seattle Mariner Harold Reynolds.
