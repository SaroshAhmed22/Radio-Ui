ABOUT US

Choice Radio is operated and maintained by Hallelujah Gospel Globally.

Franklin Record Productions was first started in 1999 that gave the vision of a Hallelujah Gospel. It is the result of God’s faithfulness and is dedicated solely to promote Gospel music from Christian talents whose hearts long to give their utmost to the Lord.

With the desire to serve communities all over the world through God’s Word and marketing skills that came from years of experience in sales, management and developing, the company’s team has their hearts set on helping God's people bring out their best and accomplish more things for the Lord's glory.

Franklin Record Productions has 25 years of recording industry experience. K-Lou Productions, its multi-platinum producer, has sold more than 75 million copies for recording artists under its umbrella.
